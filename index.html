FAQ

Q: What is the custom engraving?

A: I will print a custom name into the 3D printed stamping block, it's just cosmetic.

Q: Are the metal number and letter stamps included?

A: No, we left that out, as many bitcoiners may already have their own set. If you need one, you can grab a set off eBay like this for $30 AUD. We would suggest using the 3mm stamps as they print nice and small and neatly on the small washers.

Q: Can I order extra sets of washers, nuts and bolts?

A: Yes, sure — just add on 8,500 sats per extra set that you would like.
